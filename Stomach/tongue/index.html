This is Tongue, a blog about a challenge I am issuing myself in order to both experience new media as well as catch up on things I really should have already watched or listened to.

I'm not holding myself to any hard rules, I might miss weeks here and there or not finish one of the weekly goals, but I'll try and hopefully the habit will build.

    Here is the weekly challenge:
  • Listen to one new album
  • Watch one new movie
  • Cook one new recipe
  • And I will add anything else I want to, like if I finish a book or game and want to mention it

I will probably be posting these on Sundays so that will be the second date in the title of each post.
